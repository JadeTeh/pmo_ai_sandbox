# Project Schedule (4 Months)

## Planning Basis
- Total development effort: 240 hours
- Project duration: 4 months
- Average load: 60 hours/month (about 15 hours/week)

## Timeline and Hour Allocation
| Month | Phase | Key Activities | Hours |
|---|---|---|---:|
| Month 1 | Discovery and Architecture | Requirements finalization, system design, technical specification, backlog setup | 60 |
| Month 2 | Core Development | Core feature implementation, integration setup, unit testing | 70 |
| Month 3 | Build Completion and QA | Remaining features, bug fixing, integration testing, user acceptance preparation | 70 |
| Month 4 | Finalization and Handover | UAT support, documentation, deployment preparation, handover, training support | 40 |

Total development hours: 240

## Suggested Weekly Cadence
- Weeks 1-4 (Month 1): 15 hours/week
- Weeks 5-8 (Month 2): 17-18 hours/week
- Weeks 9-12 (Month 3): 17-18 hours/week
- Weeks 13-16 (Month 4): 10 hours/week

## Milestones
- End of Month 1: Architecture and scope baseline approved
- End of Month 2: Core feature set implemented
- End of Month 3: Feature complete and integration stable
- End of Month 4: UAT closed, documentation complete, handover done

## Risks and Buffers
- Reserve 10-15% of Month 3 and Month 4 effort for defect resolution and change requests.
- Track weekly burn rate to keep total effort within 240 hours.
