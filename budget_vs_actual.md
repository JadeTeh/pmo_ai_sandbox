## Key Variance Drivers

### Major Overruns (Over Budget)
1. **Installation Labor: +RM16,012.50 (499.69%)**
   - Scope expanded from 40 hours to 30 full calendar days
   - Estimated impact: Largest single variance driver

2. **Shipment Cost: +RM3,243.00 (300.28%)**
   - Budget assumption of 2% of hardware cost insufficient
   - Actual shipment complexity/expedited delivery required

3. **Travel Cost: +RM1,448.00 (72.40%)**
   - Additional site visits or higher transport costs than assumed

### Favorable Variances (Under Budget)
1. **Tax / Custom Duty: -RM644.00 (-19.88%)**
   - Lower-than-expected duty/tariff rates applied

2. **Pre-Sales Cost: -RM1,255.91 (-19.32%)**
   - Actual effort recorded at 8.1875 days x USD150/day

## Root Cause Analysis

### Cause 1: Installation Labor Scope Expansion
- **Original Estimate:** 40 hours (5 days)
- **Actual Requirement:** 30 full calendar days
- **Root Cause:** Project complexity, site conditions, or change orders not captured in initial estimate
- **Mitigation for Future:** Comprehensive site assessment before labor estimation

### Cause 2: Shipment Cost Underestimation
- **Original Estimate:** RM1,080 (2% of hardware)
- **Actual:** RM4,323 (8% of hardware)
- **Root Cause:** International freight, expedited shipping, or special handling requirements
- **Mitigation for Future:** Obtain carrier quotes upfront; consider duty-paid delivery terms

### Cause 3: Travel Cost Overrun
- **Original Estimate:** RM2,000 (4 trips × RM500)
- **Actual:** RM3,448
- **Root Cause:** Additional site visits, higher fuel costs, or longer travel distances
- **Mitigation for Future:** Detailed travel plan with geographical mapping

---

## Recommendations

1. **Installation Labor:** Conduct post-project review to understand scope expansion drivers. Implement change control process for future projects.

2. **Shipment Cost:** For future projects, obtain freight quotes from logistics providers before finalizing budget. Include expedited shipping buffer if timeline is tight.

3. **Travel Cost:** Develop detailed travel itinerary and budget for each phase (discovery, installation, training, post-launch support).

4. **Budget Methodology:** Keep budget and actual categories aligned (no consolidated COGS line) to avoid reporting distortions.

5. **Contingency Reserve:** Recommend 10-15% project contingency for hardware/logistics-heavy projects to absorb unforeseen costs.
